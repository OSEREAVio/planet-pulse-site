Support

Need help with Planet Pulse? Here are some common solutions:

  • Map not loading: Check your internet connection and try the refresh button in the top-right corner.
  • Location not working: Go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services and make sure Planet Pulse is allowed.
  • Restore purchase: Open the app, tap the settings icon, and tap "Restore Purchase" to restore your Pro unlock.
  • Data seems outdated: Tap the refresh button on the map screen. Earthquake data updates every few minutes, wildfires every hour.

Still need help? Reach out through the App Store listing.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Planet Pulse is designed with privacy in mind, especially for our younger users.

Data we collect:

  • Location (optional): Used only to show weather and air quality near you. Your location is never stored or shared.
  • Age group: Stored locally on your device only, to tailor content difficulty. Never sent to any server.
  • Purchase data: Managed by Apple and RevenueCat to process your Pro unlock. We do not see your payment details.

Data we do NOT collect:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No tracking or advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No data shared with third parties for advertising

Third-party services:

  • Mapbox: Provides map tiles. Subject to Mapbox Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app purchases. Subject to RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Questions about privacy? Reach out through the App Store listing.
